Lions

More about Lions!!

  • Mella

    • Mella is our zoo's fearsome lioness. While she may seem scary, Mella loves to be pet, as long as she has gotten fed!

  • Karl

    • Karl is one of the oldest animals in the zoo. The zoo is his pride and he is very territorial over his space. His roar can be hear from 10 miles away.

Monkeys

More About Monkeys!

  • Cookie

    • Cookie is a silly monkey, if he gets out of his section he likes to create havoc. Watch out for your keys!

  • Earl

    • Earl is the oldest monkey we have. He is very observant and often very comforting. He loves to cuddle!

  • Banana Pudding

    • Banana Pudding is the daughter to Earl and sister to Cookie. She is the leader of the bunch and likes to play tricks on the workers at the zoo.

Alligator

More about Alligators!

  • Wren

    • Wren is our oldest alligator. He spends most of his day in the water, and comes out when it is cool, or for food!

  • Aspen

    • Aspen is our biggest alligator. She is 14 feet tall and she has a BIG bite!

  • Mika

    • Mika is daughter to Aspen, and following in her mom's footsteps is very big. Mika loves attention from anyone!
